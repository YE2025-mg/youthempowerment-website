Project By Youth

Empowering Youth through Design Thinking

At Youth Empowerment, we focus on equipping young people with the principles of Human-Centered Design Thinking. By teaching this innovative and empathetic approach, we empower them to better understand real community needs, co-create sustainable solutions, and become proactive changemakers in their own environments.

Art, Culture and Sport

Creativity and expression meet social transformation in this vibrant sector. Here, youth use sport, artistic expression, and cultural heritage as tools for community building, peace promotion, and personal development. They are designing projects that celebrate diversity, encourage talent, and foster unity through shared experiences.

Human Rights and Civic Engagement

Rooted in the belief that every voice matters, this sector focuses on promoting democratic values, human rights awareness, and active civic participation. Young people in this sector are working on initiatives that advocate for social justice, inclusion, and youth involvement in decision-making processes.

Education and Capacity Building

This sector focuses on strengthening youth skills, knowledge, and leadership through educational initiatives and training opportunities. Our young team is preparing projects that enhance access to quality education, promote lifelong learning, and build essential life and professional skills.

Environment, Climate, and Agriculture

This sector is dedicated to empowering youth to take action on environmental sustainability, climate resilience, and agricultural innovation. Our young members are currently developing projects that promote eco-friendly practices, address climate challenges, and support sustainable farming systems. These initiatives aim to create tangible impact at both local and national levels.

Pitching Session Coming Soon!

All four sectors are working hard to design innovative, youth-led projects that respond to real community needs. These initiatives will be presented during a dedicated pitching session, where our young changemakers will showcase their ideas to potential partners, supporters, and stakeholders.

Stay tuned and be inspired by the future in the making!

Project For Youth

Digital Entrepreneurship Mentorship Program

In a world increasingly shaped by technology, young people must not only adapt but lead. Our free Digital Entrepreneurship Mentorship Program empowers youth to harness digital tools to create meaningful solutions for real-world challenges.

Through interactive workshops, hands-on mentorship, and peer collaboration, participants explore key topics such as digital business models, branding, content creation, and online marketing. The program is designed to build practical skills, spark innovation, and cultivate entrepreneurial mindsets.

By the end of the mentorship, each participant will design a digital product or service that responds to a specific need within their community. In addition to individual projects, one collective project will also be created by the entire group—an initiative designed to serve communities at a broader scale.

If you have a partnership idea or wish to support this journey, do not hesitate to contact us.
