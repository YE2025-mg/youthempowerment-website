Youth Empowerment Tackles Depression: A New Initiative Takes Flight!

Antananarivo, Madagascar

In a powerful move to address pressing social issues, the local organization Youth Empowerment is making waves, believing firmly that every societal ill has a solution, and that young people are key to finding them. This conviction was the driving force behind a recent, impactful interactive conference focused on depression, a significant social challenge affecting many Malagasy youth.

The "Care" Team from Youth Empowerment's "Sustainable Development" sector spearheaded this enriching event. Far from being a one-off, this conference marks a crucial testing phase for an upcoming, larger project aimed at combating depression among young people.

This initiative is just the beginning! Youth Empowerment invites everyone to an upcoming pitching event to learn more about this vital project and how it plans to make a lasting difference.

As the organization aptly quotes, "For souls well-born, value awaits not the number of years," highlighting the immense potential and capability of the youth they empower.

Stay tuned for more details on their upcoming pitching event and how you can support the #RaceAgainstDepression!
