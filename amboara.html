Ravoniaina Mahavalisoa Amboaranomenahy Razanadrakotomiarinjato

Ravoniaina Mahavalisoa Amboaranomenahy Razanadrakotomiarinjato, also known as Amboara, is passionately committed to empowering young people through creative, cultural, and athletic initiatives. As the Project Pilot for Art, Culture, and Sports at Youth Empowerment, she leads programs that provide young people with safe, expressive, and collaborative spaces.

She is a poet at heart and finds deep fulfillment in reading, writing, and dancing. She believes that artistic expression is not only a form of personal development, but also a powerful tool for social transformation and healing.

With a background in communication, youth journalism, and project coordination, she brings creativity, structure, and a strong sense of purpose to every initiative she supports. Her journey as a scout leader with Mpanazava eto Madagasikara has reinforced her commitment to solidarity, leadership, and service.

In everything she does, she aims to foster inclusive spaces where youth can express themselves, dream big, learn with confidence, and shape their own futures with courage and creativity.
