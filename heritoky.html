Randriamanana Heritoky Mahasedra

Vice President

Meet Randriamanana Heritoky Mahasedra. A third year lawyer student, public law course at the University of Antananarivo. Passionate about the legal system and its impact on society, he aspires to a career in the legal field, where he could use his knowledge and skills to contribute to justice and fairness.

Outside of his studies, he practices Calisthenics and Street Workout, a sporting discipline that helps develop strength, endurance and coordination. This sport also brings a feeling of well-being and discipline, values which also guide one’s studies and daily life.

Enthusiastic about creating video content, he is also convinced about the power of images and sound to tell stories, inform and entertain. Autodidact, he creates some contents for his team.

He is an ambitious, hardworking and determined person. He is eager to learn and improve, and always looking for new challenges to take on. He is convinced that his skills and motivation will allow him to succeed in his studies and his future career.
