Faneva Rezela

Ambassador of Art, Culture, and Sport

Faneva Rezela is a Business Management and Creation student at the Institute of Management of Arts and Crafts of Madagascar(IMGAM), a private institution founded in 1992 in Antananarivo, dedicated to the professional development of young entrepreneurs. His passion for entrepreneurship naturally led him to this program, allowing him to acquire the necessary foundations to manage and sustainably develop a business project.

Alongside his studies, Rezela regularly reads specialized books and takes additional business training, thus strengthening his entrepreneurial culture. This desire for continuous learning demonstrates his commitment to becoming an enlightened leader, ready to innovate and create value.

With his colleagues, he designed an innovative online platform dedicated to artworks, intended to promote Malagasy painters. This project aims to promote local art while providing artists with an online showcase, improving visibility and revenue. Currently, Rezela and his team are actively seeking a financial partner to support this ambitious initiative.

In addition to these commitments, in 2025, he took on additional responsibilities within the Youth Empowerment association, becoming an ambassador for art and creativity. In this role, he promotes art as a powerful vehicle for cultural expression and intergenerational exchange, working to preserve Malagasy cultural identity.

A visionary, Rezela aims to contribute to the recognition of Malagasy art on the national and international scene. His career, marked by academic training, community activity, and entrepreneurial innovation, makes him a promising player in Madagascar's economic and cultural development.
