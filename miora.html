Rajeriharisoa Nomenjanahary Miora

“Do what you can, with what you have, where you are.” – Theodore Roosevelt

This quote has inspired her because it reminds her that she can contribute to change no matter where she is. That’s one of the reasons she joined Youth Empowerment.

She is currently a fourth-year student in both Private Law and Political Science. She has a growing interest in project management and diplomacy, driven by a deep concern for justice and human rights.

In her free time, she enjoys reading about geopolitics and international relations. She also loves hiking and exploring nature. She is always eager to learn and is particularly interested in studying foreign languages in the future.

Her motivation lies in creating opportunities that uphold rights and dignity, especially for vulnerable communities. She is committed to learning and applying her knowledge to make meaningful contributions in the field of human rights and youth development.
