Marie Juliette Solofonihaingo

Marie Juliette Solofonihaingo is a 21-year-old active member of the Youth Empowerment association, engaged in the “Art and Culture” sector. She views art as an open, inclusive, and adaptive medium — a powerful tool that connects people and intersects with every other field.

Her passions include reading, cinema, and music, and she believes strongly in art’s ability to nourish the soul and foster understanding. She’s known for her dedication and discipline — if she were a word, it would be “principle.”

After earning her baccalaureate from Lycée Moderne Ampefiloha in 2021, Juliette pursued studies in Administration. She is currently in her third year at the Institute of Management of Arts and Trades, where she has honed her skills in structure, planning, and discipline.

Juliette is driven by the belief that “The only enemy of youth is procrastination—so long as we have time, we can achieve anything.” She hopes to use her administrative knowledge and creative spirit to advance cultural development projects in her community and beyond.
