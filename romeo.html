Roméo Tovonantenaina

President

Roméo Tovonantenaina is a dedicated young activist with a deep passion for climate action and youth empowerment. As the founder and president of Youth Empowerment, Roméo leads with a vision to inspire and mobilize young people for meaningful community impact.

At a very young age, Roméo founded the Club for Peace and Understanding during high school. This initiative, which reached approximately 300 people, was recognized with a grant from the mayor for its outstanding impact and dedication to fostering peace and mutual understanding.

Roméo currently serves as a junior law lecturer at two private universities in Madagascar. In this role, he combines academic expertise with a commitment to teaching and mentoring the next generation of legal professionals. Alongside this position, Roméo has consistently volunteered to teach university students, demonstrating a long-standing dedication to education and youth development.

Roméo holds a Master’s degree in Domestic and International Public Law, with a specialization in International Law of Human Rights and Environment. This advanced academic background underscores his commitment to addressing global challenges through legal and policy frameworks.

A proud beneficiary of the YALI Program in South Africa, Roméo acquired valuable skills in Public Management and Governance, which inform his approach to leadership and community engagement.

An expert in Human-centered Design Thinking, Roméo utilizes innovative strategies to create impactful solutions that address the needs and perspectives of individuals.

Deeply committed to climate activism and the advancement of youth participation, Roméo is dedicated to developing programs and initiatives that empower young people and promote environmental sustainability through Youth Empowerment. With a forward-thinking approach and a passion for fostering leadership among young people, Roméo guides Youth Empowerment toward a future where every young person can contribute, grow, and lead.

Aligning with the words of author and civil rights leader Martin Luther King Jr., who said, “True leadership is about empowering others to find their voice and make a lasting impact,” Roméo is committed to creating opportunities for young people to achieve their potential and make a difference in the world.
