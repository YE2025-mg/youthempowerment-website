Mitantsoa Nomenjanahary

Mitantsoa Nomenjanahary is a 20-year-old dynamic young professional with a passion for making a difference. Raised in a Christian family, she carries with her the values of kindness, compassion, and community service.

Currently a student at the École Supérieure Polytechnique d'Antananarivo (ESPA), she is pursuing a degree in Mining Engineering with a focus on Geo-Energy — specifically specializing in renewable energy sources such as geothermal and solar power. Her academic career began after earning her baccalaureate in 2023, and since then, she has already shown leadership by heading a project team of 10 for a university competition.

Her dream is to become an entrepreneur in the energy sector, helping to address Madagascar’s electricity challenges with sustainable solutions. She also envisions founding a childcare center where working parents can confidently leave their children — a project driven by her deep sense of social responsibility.

Her work ethic, leadership skills, and community-driven mindset make her a true force for good — not only in engineering but in building a future where innovation serves people first.
