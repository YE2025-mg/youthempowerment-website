Rakotoarisoa Jeannie Joséa Estelle

Rakotoarisoa Jeannie Joséa Estelle holds a Master’s degree (Year 1) in Law from the Faculty of Law and Political Science. She completed an internship at the Court of First Instance of Antananarivo, where she gained practical experience and developed a strong understanding of judicial processes.

Deeply committed to justice, she is particularly focused on human rights, especially children’s rights, the effectiveness of fundamental rights, and social law. She is convinced that law is a powerful driver of social transformation.

She currently serves as a volunteer within the Human Rights section of the Youth Empowerment Association, where she contributes to raising awareness and supporting rights-based initiatives for young people.
