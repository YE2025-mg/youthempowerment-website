RAKOTONINDRINA Marie Mirantsoa

Born on December 16, 2005, RAKOTONINDRINA Marie Mirantsoa is a passionate and engaged student currently preparing for her Baccalaureate in general education, specializing in Series L. Her journey into civic action began with joining the Youth Empowerment association as a motivated young advocate for human rights.

Mirantsoa has already demonstrated her commitment by completing the “Paikady hitondra fahombiazana goavana ara-bola” training through the Grands e-Boss course, for which she received a certificate for her participation. In 2019, she earned a gold medal and certificate of recognition in the “KINTANA MAMIRATRA” singing competition.

For Mirantsoa, knowledge of human rights is not optional — it’s essential. She strongly believes that no society can thrive if its members are unaware of their legal protections. Her mission is to educate, support, and advocate for those who face injustice or lack awareness of their rights.

She firmly states that law is not just a concept — it's the foundation of harmony in any country. Her goal is to ensure that all individuals, regardless of background, understand the rights enshrined for them by law, and are empowered to defend them.
