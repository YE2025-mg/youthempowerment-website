Andrianirina Daniel Second

Born in 1998, Andrianirina Daniel Second is a dedicated lawyer and aspiring leader with a specialization in Law and Private Business Administration. Currently enrolled in the Master 2 program in Judicial Studies at the Faculty of Law and Political Science at the University of Antananarivo (2023–2024), he integrates legal expertise with civic engagement through his work with Youth Empowerment.

Daniel’s journey is marked by consistent efforts to expand both his academic and practical impact. In 2024, he completed training in disciplinary procedures offered by the Ministry of Civil Service and participated in an anti-corruption course. He has also been trained in first aid, project management, socio-organizational dynamics, personal development, and support services, highlighting his commitment to both professional growth and human-centered leadership.

Daniel believes in the strength of collective action, advocating for the unity of young Malagasy to drive sustainable development in Madagascar. His philosophy — “Union is strength” — speaks to his conviction that only together can true and lasting change be achieved.

With his legal acumen, project experience, and heart for service, Daniel Second stands as a young changemaker bridging the gap between law, ethics, and community progress.

“Union is strength.”
