Youth Empowerment

Razafindravelo Maminirina Tahiry

Razafindravelo Maminirina Tahiry holds a Bachelor's degree in Anthropology and is currently pursuing a Master’s in Tourism Sciences. Driven by a strong passion for development and education, he dedicates his time as a project pilot to improving the lives of children in Anjeva.

As a project manager, Tahiry is committed to creating educational initiatives that provide meaningful and lasting opportunities. His mission is to make education accessible to all children in the region, while also designing vocational training programs that respond directly to the local youth’s aspirations and the job market’s needs.

Through practical, community-driven strategies, he aims to guide young people toward autonomy, empowerment, and long-term success. For Tahiry, the foundation of progress lies in equipping the next generation with both knowledge and tools to thrive sustainably.

His ultimate goal is to mobilize his experience and leadership to foster a more inclusive educational and professional ecosystem—one that ensures equal opportunities and champions local development with dignity.
