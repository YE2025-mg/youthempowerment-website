Rafenomanantsoa Oliva Sarindra

Rafenomanantsoa Oliva Sarindra is currently in her third year of French Literature studies at the University of Antananarivo, with a specialization in Cultural Mediation and Management. Her academic background has equipped her with skills in cultural communication, project coordination, and the analysis of artistic practices.

She is particularly passionate about contemporary cultural dynamics, the promotion of local heritage, and strengthening the connections between artworks, artists, and the public. Her work reflects a commitment to inclusivity, cultural diversity, and meaningful engagement through the arts.

Driven by her ambition to contribute to cultural development, Oliva aspires to design socially rooted and inclusive projects that celebrate the richness of her environment. She is currently expanding her practical experience in cultural organizations and plans to pursue further studies in cultural management or policy to refine her impact on the cultural sector.
