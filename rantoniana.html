Rantoniana Razafindrasoa

At just 23 years old, Rantoniana Razafindrasoa embodies commitment and passion for social development. With a solid academic background, she holds a professional degree in Social Work and Development, and is recognized as a social worker.

Her educational journey continues as she pursues a Master’s in Social Policies and Development, focusing on educational reform and sustainability—fields that deeply inspire her. She has also been a dedicated scout since 2010, developing a strong sense of responsibility, community, and service.

Since 2020, she has been an active volunteer with Ank’izy Association, working directly with young people. In 2023, she took on the role of facilitator for the DFC Mada program, bringing direct impact and tangible progress to communities on the ground.

Her driving principle is simple but powerful: “Education is the lever of all sectors and fields.” She is guided by inspiring quotes such as: “Be the change you wish to see in the world” and “Victory belongs to those who hold on to their values and convictions” – words that fuel her mission and persistence.

Her focus remains on fostering innovation and ensuring generational sustainability, particularly through youth empowerment and educational reform. Her involvement with Youth Empowerment is a natural extension of her passion, providing a platform to turn her ideals into lasting impact for future generations.
