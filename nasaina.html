Nasaina Andrianjarisoa

Holder of a Master's degree in Private Law from the Faculty of Law and Political Science at the University of Antananarivo, Nasaina Andrianjarisoa is a dedicated young individual actively involved in projects supporting youth.

He considers the empowerment and personal development of young people as key pillars of a nation’s progress — a vision he shares with the Youth Empowerment association. His involvement is driven by a profound belief that investing in youth creates a foundation for a more just, inclusive, and resilient future.

In his ideal world, every individual would have equal access to opportunities, regardless of their economic or social background. Nasaina is committed to helping dismantle barriers that prevent young people from fully realizing their potential.

He holds dear the values of hard work and perseverance. To him, these are more than virtues — they are cornerstones of personal growth and social progress. Beyond individual effort, he also champions empathy and a spirit of solidarity as essential forces that connect communities and enable collective advancement.

Through his role at Youth Empowerment, Nasaina works on developing sustainable projects that not only empower but also inspire others to contribute meaningfully. With integrity and purpose, he envisions a world where every young person has the power, support, and confidence to shape their own future — and by extension, the future of their communities and nation.
