Jonathan Ramiaramanana

Born on May 6, 2002 in Toamasina, Madagascar, Jonathan Ramiaramanana is a young talent from the coastal region who stands out for his bold temperament, rigor, and dynamism. Passionate about cooking, he aspires to become a great chef and maintains a deep love for literature.

After obtaining his baccalaureate, Jonathan pursued law studies at the University of Antananarivo. However, due to family circumstances, he courageously suspended his second year to pursue a new path.

He completed modular training in office computing and earned a recognized certificate. Simultaneously, he nurtured his artistic side by participating in a theater group. In 2022, he was selected as a radio actor, marking a new milestone in his creative journey.

Jonathan later began working in remote monitoring while continuing his active job search. His professional goals include achieving financial independence, securing a stable, well-paid job, and eventually starting his own business to create job opportunities for others.

His generosity and altruism drive his community-oriented ambitions. Guided by his personal motto:

"Everything is possible for those who dream, dare, work and never give up."
