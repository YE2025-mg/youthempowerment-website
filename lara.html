Kong Moulee Lara Tinah Raharivony

Born on June 16, 2005, Kong Moulee Lara Tinah Raharivony has pursued academic studies in law and foreign languages after earning her diploma in the literary track. As a passionate human rights advocate, she joined the Youth Empowerment association to deepen her civic engagement.

In 2023, Lara completed a program in computer science and was awarded a certificate in January 2024. She then completed her first year of law and worked with Maarif International School Madagascar to distribute French literary books across several campuses.
