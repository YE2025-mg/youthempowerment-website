Nanie Seheno Razafindrabe

Nanie Seheno Razafindrabe is a 22-year-old Malagasy woman with a passion for business management and administration, specializing in Transit and Customs. She is currently pursuing her studies in International Commerce, while working as a salesperson in a transit company involved in import and export operations, where she also oversees logistics and coordination.

Since her teenage years, Nanie has nurtured a fascination for international trade and strategic business management. This passion naturally led her to specialize in these complementary fields, combining academic excellence with real-world application.

Alongside her studies, Nanie has accumulated practical experience through multiple internships, where she has strengthened her skills in business strategy, administrative procedures, and global operations.

Through her academic background and professional commitments, Nanie aspires to become an influential leader in international business. She aims to shape responsible global trade practices while promoting ethical, inclusive, and sustainable models. Her goal is to help companies thrive in an increasingly complex and interconnected world.
