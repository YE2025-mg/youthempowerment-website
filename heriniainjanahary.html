Heriniainjanahary My Life

Heriniainjanahary My Life embodies passion and innovation as a leading young entrepreneur in Madagascar. As the founder of My Auto, a prominent car repair company, and PIE Madagascar, which specializes in electrical installations, plumbing, and IT, he skillfully merges his technical expertise with managerial acumen to address Madagascar’s unique challenges.

Before launching his ventures, Heriniainjanahary served as an Internal Controller for a major corporate group in Madagascar, where he developed a strong foundation in management, auditing, and internal control. His educational background in Business Management, combined with certifications in Audit, Internal Control, and Project Management, underscores his commitment to transparency and impactful leadership.

Heriniainjanahary’s entrepreneurial journey is driven by his passion for cars, a keen interest in new technologies, and a deep commitment to sustainability, social equity, and accessibility to technology. He aims to develop My Auto into a national leader in automotive maintenance and establish PIE Madagascar as a top contender in its specialized fields.

His ultimate goal is to become an accomplished multi-entrepreneur and business consultant for startups and SMEs, helping the next generation of entrepreneurs realize their business visions and make a positive impact on society.
