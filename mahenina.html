Randriambahoaka Barinjanahary Mahenina

Randriambahoaka Barinjanahary Mahenina is a dedicated volunteer at Youth Empowerment Madagascar. With a passion for community service and inclusion, Mahenina contributes time, energy, and empathy to youth-focused initiatives that create opportunities for growth and development.

Mahenina believes deeply in recognizing the unique strengths of every individual, and ensuring that no one is underestimated because of conventional expectations or limited perceptions. This mindset reflects his guiding principle — to help others thrive in their own environment and on their own terms.

As a volunteer, he offers both his commitment and creativity to building a more inclusive future for young people in Madagascar, where diversity of talent and character are celebrated.

“Don't judge a fish by its ability to climb a tree.”
